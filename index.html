RPA Camp

What is RPA? Robotic process automation (or RPA) is a form of business process automation technology based on metaphorical software robots (bots) or on artificial intelligence (AI)/digital workers. It is sometimes referred to as software robotics (not to be confused with robot software).

…but why?

Robotic process automation (RPA). It’s a type of software that enables automation of even complex digital processes by performing them much in the way that human users would perform them.

Why UiPath?

UiPath is a Windows-based desktop Robotic Process Automation software tool architects to automate business processes within Enterprises. This tool enables organizations to execute processes more efficiently by automating monotonous and repetitive tasks.
